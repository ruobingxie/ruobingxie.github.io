Bio

Ruobing Xie is a senior researcher of Tencent. He received his BEng degree in 2014 and his master degree in 2017 from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University. His research interests include Large Language Model, Recommender System, Natural Language Processing, and Information Retrieval. He has published 100+ papers in top-tier conferences and journals including ACL, KDD, SIGIR, NeurIPS, and AAAI, with 7000+ citations according to Google Scholar. He has received the first prize of the Natural Science Award (Science and Technology) of the Ministry of Education, RecSys 2024 Best Full Paper, RecSys 2023 Best Short Paper, the Paper Award Nominations of TheWebConf 2023 (spotlight), and CCIR 2024 best paper candidate. He is supported by Scholar of Young Talent Promoting Project of CAST. He is a member of Chinese Information Processing Society of China, Youth Working Committee & Social Media Processing Committee & Information Retrieval Committee, and is in the World's Top 2 % Scientists 2024. He serves as AC/SPC/PC of ACL, EMNLP, NAACL, KDD, WWW, SIGIR, ICLR, NeurIPS, AAAI, IJCAI, etc.
